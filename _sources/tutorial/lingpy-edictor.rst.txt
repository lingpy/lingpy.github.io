=============================================================
Computer-Assisted Language Comparison with LingPy and EDICTOR
=============================================================

Two recent tutorials provide more information on how LingPy can be combined with additional tools:

* List, J.-M. (2017): **Historical language comparison with LingPy and EDICTOR. Tutorial presented at the Mini-Bayesian School for Transeurasian Linguists** (Max Planck Institute for the Science of Human History, Jena, 2017/11/06-09). `[URL] <https://github.com/digling/edictor-tutorial>`_

* List, J.-M. (2017): **Sequence comparison with LingPy. Tutorial presented at the Quantitative Methods Spring School** (Max Planck Institute for the Science of Human History, Jena, 2017/05/08-14). `[URL] <https://github.com/shh-dlce/qmss-2017/tree/master/LingPy>`_
